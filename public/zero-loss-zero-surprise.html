üîí Zero Loss Principle

Your data, your control. The Codex project is built on the foundational principle that users should never lose ownership, access, or control over their data and digital assets.

Data Ownership Guarantees

  • You own your events: All logged events, analytics data, and earned Sentient Cents belong to you
  • Portable data: Export your complete data history in standard formats (CSV, JSON) at any time
  • Immutable ledger: Your contributions are recorded in cryptographically verifiable proof ledgers
  • No vendor lock-in: All tools and schemas are open-source and interoperable
