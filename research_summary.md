### Ridgefield Conservatory of Dance (RCD)

- **Website**: ridgefielddance.org
- **Address**: 440-444 Main Street, Ridgefield, CT
- **Structure**: 501(c)(3) nonprofit with Board of Directors
- **Leadership**: Amy Piantaggini (Executive Director), Polly Kingsbury (Educational Director)
- **Philosophy**: Technique-first, artistry, non-competitive; nurtures "thinking artists"
- **Programs**: Strong ballet/modern core, jazz, tap, hip-hop; levels-based syllabus; pre-professional training; Nutcracker, Spring Concert
- **Focus**: Discipline, creativity, community outreach; minimal competition emphasis

### Key Comparison

- RCD: Ideal for classical technique and artistic development in a nonprofit setting.
- DFR: Better for competitive tracks, broader styles (e.g., acro), and wellness integration in a for-profit model.
- Both: Participate in local Nutcracker; foster confidence and high-quality training. Complementary rather than direct rivals.
